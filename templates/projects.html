Vision & Priorities

"For many years, New Yorkers have enjoyed the great benefits of living in this magnificent state. Unfortunately, previous administrations have neglected some important areas of the government. As a result, we are witnessing how people struggle to take care of their basic needs. William G. is committed to restoring those areas of the system where immediate change is a priority."

Strong Economy

Although the economy of the State of New York has been stable in the last decade, There is room for improvement. No only the management of tax revenues can be handled more effectively, but also increased in the first year of operation. Those funds can then be used towards different areas where a budged increase is mostly needed. These changes will facilitate growth in the economy turning stagnating wages into competitive salaries for all New Yorkers.

Public Education System

One of the areas that we consider to be our number one priority is public education, especially for our kids. If we want New York to thrive, we must invest heavily in the future of every single kid in our state. That is why we have focused our attention to solve the problem that lack of funding causes in certain areas to local schools in the state of New York. Safety

Despite a continuous increase of population, New York State consistently ranks among the top ten safest states in the United States. Violent crimes in this state have dropped over 15% in the last decade and latest reports indicate that murder rates in 2017 were the lowest since 1963 when records started to be filed. Although these are positive news, William G. still believes we can do more. So his plan to keep safety at its highest level is to increase well-trained police agents in places where it is necessary and launch an educational campaign where all New York citizens learn the importance of public responsibility.
